Who We Are

Career services built for Bangladesh

NextStep Resume & Portfolio has been providing career services across Bangladesh since 2024. In that time, we've successfully completed 235+ services for clients — including resume writing, portfolio design, LinkedIn optimization, English speaking training, and AI skills coaching.

We work with fresh graduates, early-career professionals, and anyone looking to present themselves better to recruiters — whether that's a first job, a career switch, or an opportunity abroad.

Our mission is simple: make every job seeker in Bangladesh competitive with a globally-standard resume and portfolio, regardless of their background.

Fuad Bin Yousuf

Founder & Manager, Business Development

Fuad is the founder of NextStep Resume & Portfolio. Alongside running NextStep, he currently works as a Business Development Manager at an IT company. Having worked closely with recruiters and hiring processes, he saw how often talented candidates were overlooked simply because they didn't have the right documents to represent them. That gap is why he started NextStep — to give every job seeker in Bangladesh the tools to compete on a global standard.
